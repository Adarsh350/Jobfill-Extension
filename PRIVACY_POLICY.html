Privacy Policy for JobFill Extension

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Overview

JobFill is a free, privacy-first Chrome extension that autofills job application forms. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Collection & Storage

Personal Information You Provide

JobFill collects the following information only when you explicitly enter it in the extension popup:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location (city/state/country)
  • Work authorization status
  • LinkedIn profile URL
  • Years of experience
  • Education details
  • Work experience
  • Skills
  • Custom answers to job application questions
  • Resume file (PDF/DOCX)

How We Store Data

⚡ Key Point: All data is stored exclusively in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync and chrome.storage.local APIs. No data is ever sent to any external server.
  • ✅ 100% offline-first — no external requests
  • ✅ No data sharing with third parties
  • ✅ No analytics or tracking
  • ✅ No telemetry
  • ✅ No cookies
  • ✅ You remain in complete control

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  1. Auto-fill job application forms on supported ATS platforms
  2. Auto-attach your resume to file inputs in job forms
  3. Match your saved answers to application questions using local keyword matching

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Marketing or advertising
  • Selling or trading to third parties
  • Profiling or targeting
  • Machine learning or AI training
  • Any purpose other than autofilling forms

Supported Platforms

The extension can fill forms on these job application platforms:

  • Greenhouse ATS
  • Lever ATS
  • Workday
  • Ashby ATS
  • iCIMS
  • LinkedIn Jobs (Easy Apply)
  • Bayt.com
  • Generic fallback for other ATS systems

Chrome Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — Required to store your profile data locally
  • scripting — Required to inject autofill logic into job pages
  • activeTab — Required to know which tab is active
  • tabs — Required to access tab information

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  1. Clicking the JobFill extension icon
  2. Going to the Settings tab
  3. Clicking Clear All Data
  4. Or uninstalling the extension — all data is automatically deleted

Security

  • Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in security measures
  • Your data is only accessible to your Chrome profile on your device
  • We cannot access your data — it never leaves your computer
  • The extension implements a strict Content Security Policy

Third-Party Services

JobFill uses ZERO third-party services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Error tracking (Sentry, etc.)
  • Session recording
  • Event tracking
  • Cookies or tracking pixels

Children's Privacy

JobFill is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on GitHub with an updated "Effective Date".

Contact & Support

For questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy practices:

  • GitHub: github.com/Adarsh350/jobfill-extension
  • Issues: Open an issue on GitHub for support questions

Summary

In plain English: JobFill stores everything you put into it on your computer, nowhere else. We don't see your data, we don't use it for anything other than filling forms on job sites you choose to visit, and we don't share it with anyone. If you delete the extension, your data is gone.
